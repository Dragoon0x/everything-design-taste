# Real Estate Design

## Property Search
- Map-based search with draw-to-search.
- Filter: price range, bedrooms, bathrooms, square footage, property type.
- Sort: price, newest, relevance.
- Save search with notification for new listings.

## Listing Page
- Photo gallery (20+ photos for serious listings).
- Key facts: price, bedrooms, bathrooms, sqft, year built.
- Map with neighborhood context.
- School ratings, walkability score, commute times.
- Agent contact with response time.
- Schedule viewing button.
- Price history and comparable sales.
- Mortgage calculator.

## Virtual Tours
- 360° photos or video walkthrough.
- Floor plan with room labels.
- Measure tool for furniture planning.
- Works on mobile without special app.
