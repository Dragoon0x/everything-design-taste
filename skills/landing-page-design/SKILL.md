# Landing Page Design

## Above the Fold
1. **Headline:** Clear value proposition (what you get, not what it is)
2. **Subheadline:** Supporting detail or social proof
3. **Visual:** Screenshot, demo, or relevant image
4. **CTA:** Single, clear action button
5. **Trust signal:** Logo bar, stat, or testimonial

## Section Flow
1. Hero (value prop + CTA)
2. Social proof (logos, numbers, testimonial)
3. Problem statement (what's broken today)
4. Solution (how you fix it, with visuals)
5. Features/benefits (3-4 key ones)
6. Testimonials/case studies
7. Pricing (if applicable)
8. FAQ
9. Final CTA (repeat the hero CTA)

## CTA Rules
- One primary CTA per page (repeated at key points)
- Specific: 'Start free trial' not 'Get started'
- Contrasting color from the rest of the page
- Above the fold AND at the bottom
- No competing secondary CTAs near the primary

## Testing Priorities
1. Headline (biggest impact)
2. CTA text and placement
3. Hero image/video
4. Social proof type and placement
5. Page length
