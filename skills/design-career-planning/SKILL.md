## Specialization
| Specialty | Focus |
|-----------|-------|
| Product Design | End-to-end product experience |
| Visual/Brand | Visual identity and aesthetics |
| UX Research | User understanding and evidence |
| Interaction Design | Behavior and motion |
| Design Systems | Components and tooling |
| Content Design | Language and information |
| Design Engineering | Code and design intersection |

## Skill Development Strategy
1. **T-shaped:** Deep in one area, broad in adjacent areas
2. **Deliberate practice:** Work on weaknesses, not strengths
3. **Build in public:** Portfolio, writing, speaking develops your thinking
4. **Seek feedback:** From people better than you, not just peers
5. **Teach:** Mentoring juniors accelerates your own growth
