# Social Media Content

## Platform Guidelines

### X (Twitter)
- Thread format: hook → story → insight → CTA.
- Hook tweet: question, bold claim, or surprising fact.
- 1-2 line paragraphs. White space matters.
- Images: 1200x675px (16:9).

### LinkedIn
- Professional insights, not corporate speak.
- Personal stories outperform thought leadership.
- First 2 lines must hook (before "see more").
- Carousel posts for frameworks and lists.

### Instagram
- Visual-first. Caption supports the image.
- Carousel for educational content (10 slides).
- Stories for behind-the-scenes and engagement.
- Reels for reach (vertical video, 15-60s).

## Content Principles
1. One idea per post.
2. Specifics beat generalities.
3. Share what you learned, not what you know.
4. Ask questions to drive engagement.
5. Consistency > virality.
