# Testimonial Display

## Types
| Type | Credibility | Design |
|------|-----------|--------|
| Quote + name + role | High | Card with photo |
| Star rating + text | Medium-High | Review card |
| Logo wall | Medium | Row of logos |
| Case study | Highest | Detailed story |
| Metric ("10K+ users") | Medium | Large number |
| Video testimonial | Very High | Video embed |

## Rules
1. Real names and companies (not "John D.")
2. Photos of real people (not stock)
3. Specific outcomes ("Reduced churn by 23%" not "Great product")
4. Relevant to the page context (pricing page → ROI testimonials)
5. 3-5 testimonials is enough (more dilutes impact)
6. Place near CTAs and decision points
