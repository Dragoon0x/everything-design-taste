# Pricing Strategy Design

## Model Types
| Model | Best For | Example |
|-------|----------|----------|
| Flat rate | Simple products, clear value | Basecamp |
| Tiered | Feature differentiation | Slack, Notion |
| Per-seat | Team products | GitHub, Figma |
| Usage-based | Variable consumption | AWS, Twilio |
| Freemium | Network effects, viral growth | Spotify, Dropbox |
| Hybrid | Complex products | Salesforce |

## Psychology
1. **Anchor high:** Show expensive plan first
2. **Charm pricing:** $9.99 vs $10 (works for B2C, not B2B)
3. **Annual discount:** Show monthly cost + 'save 20%' for annual
4. **Decoy effect:** Middle plan is the best value by design
5. **Loss aversion:** 'You'll lose access to...' on downgrade

## Design Rules
- 3 tiers maximum for most products
- Highlight the recommended plan visually
- Free tier: generous enough to be useful, limited enough to drive upgrade
- Enterprise: 'Contact sales' with key differentiators listed
- Show total annual cost AND monthly equivalent
