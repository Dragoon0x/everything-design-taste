# Media Entertainment Design

## Content Discovery
- **Rows/rails** of categorized content (Netflix pattern).
- **Featured/Hero:** Auto-playing preview with title, description, play button.
- **Personalization:** "Because you watched..." "Trending in [genre]".
- **Continue watching:** Most important row, easily accessible.

## Media Player
- Minimal controls that appear on hover/tap, disappear on idle.
- Scrubbing: preview thumbnails on hover over timeline.
- Quality selector, subtitles, volume, fullscreen.
- Mobile: tap to pause, double-tap to skip 10s.
- Keyboard: space=pause, arrows=skip, M=mute, F=fullscreen.

## Content Pages
- Hero artwork + title + metadata (year, rating, duration).
- Description: 2-3 lines, expandable.
- Episodes/seasons for series.
- Similar content recommendations.
- User actions: play, add to list, rate, share.
