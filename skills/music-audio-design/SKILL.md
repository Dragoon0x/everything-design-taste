# Music Audio Design

## Player Controls
- Always visible (bottom bar or mini-player).
- Play/pause, skip, previous, progress bar, volume.
- Album art visible at a glance.
- Expand to full-screen player.
- Queue management: view, reorder, add, remove.

## Browsing & Discovery
- Personalized home: recently played, recommended, new releases.
- Genre/mood browsing.
- Artist pages: bio, discography, related artists.
- Playlist covers that reflect content.

## Playlist Design
- Track list: number, title, artist, album, duration.
- Drag to reorder.
- Currently playing track highlighted.
- Add to playlist from anywhere (context menu, drag, share).
- Collaborative playlists: shared editing.

## Audio-Specific
- Waveform visualization for podcasts/audiobooks.
- Speed control (0.5x to 2x).
- Sleep timer.
- Download for offline.
- Resume where left off (per track).
