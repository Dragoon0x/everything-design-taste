# Travel Hospitality Design

## Search
- Destination + dates + guests as primary inputs.
- Flexible dates option.
- Recent searches and saved searches.
- Map view alongside list view.

## Booking Flow
1. Search results with key info (price, rating, distance).
2. Detail page with full photos, amenities, policies.
3. Selection/customization (room type, extras).
4. Guest information.
5. Payment.
6. Confirmation with booking reference.

## Itinerary
- Day-by-day view with timeline.
- Map with all locations pinned.
- Reservation details accessible offline.
- Shareable with travel companions.

## Trust
- Real photos (labeled "actual photos" vs "stock").
- Verified reviews with recency indicators.
- Cancellation policy clearly stated before booking.
- Total price upfront (no hidden fees at checkout).
