# Print Design For Digital

## What Translates
- **Grid systems** — print grids work digitally with responsive adaptation.
- **Typographic hierarchy** — same principles, different execution.
- **White space** — equally powerful on screen.
- **Visual rhythm** — alternating density creates interest.

## What Doesn't
- **Fixed dimensions** — screens aren't fixed. Design for flow.
- **CMYK color** — convert to RGB/HEX. Colors will shift.
- **Columns of text** — multi-column body text doesn't work on mobile.
- **Bleed and trim** — no physical edges on screen.

## Adapting Print Techniques
- **Pull quotes:** Work as full-width interruptions in scroll.
- **Drop caps:** Work on desktop, simplify for mobile.
- **Caption placement:** Consider responsive reflow.
- **Photo essays:** Translate to scroll-based storytelling.
- **Spreads:** Translate to full-viewport sections.
